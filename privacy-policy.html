Independent, private, and free to use

Privacy, without the fine print.

Time conversion happens in your browser. Retime does not collect your browsing activity or send page content to us.

Retime does not collect, sell, or share your data.

No profiles, no analytics, no advertising, and no record of the pages you visit.

What Retime sees

On pages where you enable it, Retime temporarily reads visible text to find and convert times. That work happens on your device. The text is not saved or sent anywhere.

What gets saved

Your time zones, appearance, site access choices, and other preferences are saved with Chrome storage. If Chrome Sync is enabled, Google may sync those settings between your signed-in browsers. Retime never receives them.

What leaves your browser

Nothing from the pages you visit is sent to us. Retime has no analytics, tracking pixels, ads, remote logging, or server used for time conversion.

Optional support

If you choose to contribute, you leave Retime and pay on Stripe's website. Stripe handles the information you enter there. Retime never sees your full card number or payment details. Stripe's privacy policy applies.

Bug reports and email

The report-a-bug links open your email app. Nothing is sent until you choose to send the message, and we only receive what you put in that email.

Your control

You can change site access and saved preferences in Retime at any time. Removing the extension removes its access. Chrome manages synced settings under your Google account.
